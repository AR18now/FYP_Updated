1.2 Scope
The Purrfect Assistant project is a holistic solution to cat care that will help owners to
easily manage and optimize their pets’ health and wellbeing. This application is meant
to help users keep track of their cat’s diet, health, behavior and daily activities, with
personalized recommendations and real time updates to improve their overall care expe-
rience. Through artificial intelligence, the app will use personalized data of your own cat
to deliver personalized suggestions just for you. This will significantly improve health
outcomes and foster a much closer relationship between a cat and its owner. This mobile
application is aimed at cat owners who look for a comprehensive solution for daily care
and health of their cat, easy and convenient for both the novice and experienced pet own-
ers, with a focus on user friendliness and accuracy of data, as well as habit formation and
behavioral tracking, so that cat owners can better take care of their cats. Purrfect Assistant
app has the main aim to be the best tool for cat owners, aiding in proactive management
of their pets’ health and well-being in a convenient way, so much so that the owners feel
it becoming a necessity for them.

System Overview
Purrfect Assistant is a mobile app that offers all in one care and management for cat own-
ers. The app is designed to help cats to be healthier and more well, by providing users
with tools to track their pet’s diet, health, behaviour and daily activity. Purrfect Assis-
tant is a more personalized way to connect cats with their owners through personalized
recommendations and real time updates to make sure their care needs are met.
Artificial intelligence is used by the app to analyze personalized data to help users opti-
mize cat health outcomes. Purrfect Assistant makes the app user friendly and easy to use
for both novice and experienced pet owners, making it a need to have for proactive cat
care management.
Background and Context: In the current times, pet ownership is increasing and cat
owners are looking for more effective tools to manage their pets’ health and wellbeing.
However, many owners find it difficult to provide adequate care because there are no
personalized resources and information that are specifically for their particular needs.
This gap is addressed by Purrfect Assistant, an AI based solution which integrates modern
technology and cat care management
Functionality: It is structured in several core modules such as Breed detection, Training
and Care, Health and Nutrition, Accessories, a Chat Bot for immediate assistance and a
GPS Tracker to track a cat’s location. The modules are created with a view to cover each
aspect of cat ownership and with a holistic approach to caring for your feline companion.
Design: The frontend will be built in Flutter for cross platform support across both an-
droid and IOS. The backend will use a Node.js server to implement business logic for all
the modules. It will also use a Hugging Face AI model for the chat bot and a Tensorflow
machine learning model for breed detection. The database will be maintained on Firebase.
Purrfect Assistant combines technology with pet care knowledge, hoping to be a game
changer for how cat owners interact and manage their pets’ health and well being. The
app is not only a tool, but also a companion to every cat owner, improving cats’ quality of

3.3 Data Design
3.3 Data Design
The Purrfect Assistant app takes its information domain from unstructured data to struc-
tured data, transforming it into well defined data models that can be stored and processed
within Firebase, a cloud based NoSQL database. Using this approach, we can retrieve
data smoothly, scale, and update real time, which improves the user experience and pro-
vides the means for managing cat care information in an effective manner.
The major data items that need to be stored will be:
1. User profiles
2. Cat profiles
3. Nutrition plans
4. Symptom analysis reports
5. Chat bot interactions
Firebase’s real time database is mostly used for data processing inside the app. The app
processes and stores the data corresponding to when users interact with the app, for ex-
ample when uploading images for breed detection or logging symptoms. With Firebase,
it is real time features that make it possible to get the latest information instantly across
devices.
