ProblemStatement
The previous year proved bleak for various industries and companies in Pakistan, withseveral expectingto shut down and suffer huge losses this year [1]. Amidst this crisis, arises the need to cut down onvarious expenses, including the cost spent during hiring processes in the business sector. The averageprice of the hiring process has risen to$4,000[2]. Thereareseveral methodsinvolvedinahiringprocesswhich take a lot of time and cost to be executed. Unfortunately, morethanhalf of theresourcesspent arewasted.
A company typically receives thousands of CVs once a job opening is announced. The HRdepartmenthastorevieweachresumeandfilter themost qualifiedonesamongall applicants. Out of thethousands, atleast ahundredapplicantsareselectedfor interviews. Theinterviewingprocesscantakedaystoconclude.Sometimes, some applicants are called for multiple interviews to ensure their credibility. However, theproblemlies in the consumptionof timeandresourcesduringthisprocess. Electricitycostsaremultipliedand a portion of the company’s assets are consumed. Most of all, time is money. These assets and timecanbeutilizedfor someproductiveactivitieswithinthecompany.
Lastly, a common concern regarding the hiring process is nepotism and biases. In many situations,competent andeligibleapplicantsfor ajobvacancyfail tosecureaninterview. Discriminatorypracticesinrecruitment and hiring are an ongoing issue that is aconcernnot just for workplacerelations, but alsofora wider understanding of economic justice and inequality [3]. This can lead to unfair selections,completelyneglectingthemerit.

Users search for the HireUp Website Application on the browser. They will open the application and beable to log in as a recruiter or an applicant. They can sign uptoget accesstotheapplicationif theyhavenot alreadycreatedanaccount.
As applicants, users will be asked to enter personal information, including email, phone number, dateofbirth, and location.
