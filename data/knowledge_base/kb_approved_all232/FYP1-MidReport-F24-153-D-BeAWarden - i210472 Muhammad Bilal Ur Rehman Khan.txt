## 1. Introduction

1. Introduction
Increasing Accidents: Traffic violations cause a lot of accidents. Every year there are
roughly 10 thousand accidents of which 50 percent are fatal and this percentage is growing
every year. Strict traffic supervision can control these accidents.
1.2 Scope
The scope of the project basically focuses on providing an AI-powered traffic violation
system that enables registered users to act as virtual wardens. Our users will be able
to record certain violations through our app. They will become registered users or vir-
tual wardens after passing a test. Our App will provide an interface to record violations
through their mobile phones fixed in the car . The videos will be sent to centralized
server for violation detection through AI. Then using licence plate recognition system
the violator will be tracked and the authorities will be notified and then a challan will be
sent to the violator by the authorities.Basically this system is providing help by utilizing
dynamic cameras used by virtual wardens. The system will cover a wide range of viola-
tions described in the modules. The data privacy will be taken care of. Future expansion
possibilities include advanced AI algorithms to detect complex traffic behaviors and also
integration of our system with smart city cameras and system.

2.3 Non-Functional Requirements
2.3.1 Reliability
The system shall have a MTBF of at least 200 hours of continuous operation. Our sys-
tem shall have a downtime of no more than 2 hours for usual maintenance of server and
new updates, so we can ensure complete availability of server to user fro working. The
recorded videos shall be uploaded to the server without any corruption. Backup shall be
kept in the app if any corruption of video occurs.

The system shall offer an appealing UI that shall allow users to record and upload videos
easily. The voice command feature for recording violation video chunks shall have an
accuracy rate of at least 90%, and users shall receive clear feedback for successful or
failed commands. The app will be very easy to use.

All personal data such as videos, user information, and violation reports shall be secured.
Unauthorized access to uploaded videos or violation data must be prevented using secure
and proper authentication and role-based access controls. The app access will be given to
only authorized users.

System Overview
In our App , The user will open our app and fix the device on the dashboard .The device
will record the video . When the user comes across a violation he will use voice command
to save the chunk of the video in which the violation will be captured . The video chunk
will be saved locally in the phone storage. Then the user will upload all captured videos
to the server. The server will run an AI model for all violation detection using a pipeline.
In the end if any violation detected we will use OCR to get the license plate and notify
the authorities and the user .
