1.2 Scope
The scope of this project is to develop an AI-based system for the detection and seg-
mentation of lung, breast, and skin cancers. The system will employ advanced deep
learning techniques such as Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs) and Transformers to
perform accurate segmentation of cancerous regions in medical images, specifically using
CT scans for lung cancer, mammograms for breast cancer, and 2D images for skin can-
cer. Additionally, the system will estimate the size of cancerous nodules or lesions using
radiomic features and predict the risk of cancer progression in the lungs. The web-based
interface will enable medical professionals to interact with the models, view detected
cancerous areas, collaborate with colleagues, and generate comprehensive diagnostic re-
ports. The platform will also support 3D visualization tools for interacting with CT scan
images, secure patient record-keeping, data encryption, and APIs for integration with
hospital management systems and electronic health records (EHR). The solution aims
to enhance diagnostic accuracy, streamline workflows, and foster collaboration among
healthcare professionals, thereby improving patient outcomes.
1.3 Modules
The system is divided into various modules for different functionalities and features in the
multi-cancer detection system. The modules are organized regarding the types of cancer
detection and extra functionality, which is provided by the system.
1.3.1 Module 1:Cancer Detection and Segmentation
In this module, models will be applied to detect and segment lung, breast, and skin can-
cers, each based on various kinds of imaging techniques with different machine learning
models.
Lung Cancer Detection and Segmentation : Utilize CT Scans for the detection and
segmentation of lungs, further segmenting the cancerous regions in order to train the
models on nodule size estimation using the detected radiomic features.
Breast Cancer Detection and Segmentation :Breast cancer detection and segmentation
by mammograms, estimation of size of nodules with the use of radiomic features.
