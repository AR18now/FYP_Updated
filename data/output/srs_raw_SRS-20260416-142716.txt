INTRODUCTION
Purpose: To develop an AI-based system for detecting plant diseases from leaf images uploaded by farmers.
Scope: This document specifies the requirements for the AI-Based Disease Detection System. It covers functionalities related to image analysis, treatment suggestions, and historical tracking but excludes aspects such as security, scalability, and maintainability.
Definitions/Acronyms:
- AI: Artificial Intelligence
- ML: Machine Learning
References: IEEE Std 830-1998, IEEE Standard for Software Requirements Specifications
Overview: The system is designed to assist farmers in managing their crops more effectively by providing timely information about potential diseases.

OVERALL DESCRIPTION
Product Perspective: The product is intended for agricultural users who need quick and accurate identification of plant diseases. The target audience includes small-scale and large-scale farmers.
Product Functions:
- Image Upload: Allow farmers to upload leaf images via a mobile or web application.
- Image Analysis: Use ML models to detect diseases present in the uploaded images.
- Treatment Suggestions: Provide recommendations based on identified diseases.
- Historical Tracking: Maintain records of past analyses for individual users.
- Analytics: Offer insights into crop health trends over time.
User Characteristics: Users are expected to have basic computer literacy and access to either a smartphone or desktop computer.
Constraints: The system must operate efficiently even under limited network conditions.
Assumptions/Dependencies: The system relies on a pre-existing database of labeled leaf images for training purposes.

SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS
External Interface Requirements:
- User Interface: Mobile app interface and web portal for uploading images.
- Hardware Interface: Compatibility with various smartphones and computers.
- Software Interface: Integration with existing farm management systems if available.
- Communication Interface: Support for slow internet connections without compromising functionality.
Functional Requirements:
FR-01: Image Upload
Input: Leaf images captured by farmers.
Processing: Store images temporarily until processed.
Output: Confirmation message upon successful upload.
Priority: Medium
FR-02: Image Analysis
Input: Uploaded leaf images.
Processing: Apply ML algorithms to classify images as healthy or diseased.
Output: Identification of disease(s) along with a confidence score.
Priority: High
FR-03: Treatment Suggestions
Input: Identified disease(s).
Processing: Retrieve relevant treatment advice from a knowledge base.
Output: List of suggested treatments or preventive measures.
Priority: High
FR-04: Historical Tracking
Input: User-specific data.
Processing: Record details of each image analysis session.
Output: Storage of historical records accessible to the user.
Priority: Low
FR-05: Analytics
Input: Historical data from user sessions.
Processing: Generate reports on crop health trends.
Output: Visualizations showing changes in crop health over time.
Priority: Medium
Non-functional Requirements:
Usability: The system shall be easy to navigate and understand for non-expert users.
Reliability: Ensure consistent accuracy in disease detection across different types of devices and networks.
Performance: Deliver results within a reasonable timeframe regardless of network speed.
Portability: Function well on both mobile and desktop platforms.

SYSTEM FEATURES
Feature 1: Image Upload
- Allows farmers to upload leaf images directly from their device's camera or photo gallery.
- Ensures compatibility with a wide range of smartphone and computer formats.

Feature 2: Image Analysis
- Utilizes advanced ML techniques to accurately diagnose plant diseases.
- Provides detailed explanations of how the diagnosis was made.

Feature 3: Treatment Suggestions
- Offers tailored solutions based on the specific disease detected.
- Includes links to external resources for further reading.

Feature 4: Historical Tracking
- Maintains a comprehensive log of all past analyses performed by the farmer.
- Enables users to review previous findings easily.

Feature 5: Analytics
- Generates visual summaries of crop health status over time.
- Helps farmers make informed decisions regarding future farming practices.
