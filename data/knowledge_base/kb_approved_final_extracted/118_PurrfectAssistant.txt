SOFTWARE REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (IEEE 830)
Project: PurrfectAssistant - Comprehensive Cat Care Mobile App
================================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This document outlines the requirements for the Purrfect Assistant mobile application, an all-in-one solution for cat care, including diet tracking, breed detection, health monitoring, and a GPS tracker.

1.2 Scope
The app aims to help cat owners manage their pet's health, diet, and overall care. Features include breed detection via image upload, personalized training and care recommendations (including special care for newborns/pregnant cats), health and nutrition management (diet tracking, symptom analysis, vet recommendations), a cat accessory finder, an AI-powered CatBot for quick questions, and a GPS tracker for locating cats via a custom collar.

1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, Abbreviations
- AI: Artificial Intelligence
- GPS: Global Positioning System
- ML: Machine Learning

2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
2.1 Product Perspective
PurrfectAssistant provides a personalized, all-in-one solution to help cat owners proactively manage their pets' health and well-being, integrating AI for personalized suggestions.

2.2 Product Functions (Summary)
- Breed Detection: Identify cat breed from uploaded pictures.
- Training and Care: Personalized recommendations for training, grooming, exercise, and special care.
- Health and Nutrition: Personalized nutrition plans, diet tracking, symptom analysis, vet clinic recommendations.
- Accessories: Web-scraped accessory listings with price filters.
- CatBot: AI-powered chatbot for quick answers.
- GPS Tracker: Real-time location tracking via a custom collar.

2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
- Cat Owners: Primary caregivers seeking personalized care solutions.
- Veterinarians: Medical professionals who can use the app for assistance and sponsor their clinics.
- Cat Accessory Sellers: Retailers who can sponsor and promote their products.

3. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Breed Detection
FR1: The app shall allow users to use the built-in phone camera to capture cat images within the app.
FR2: The app shall allow users to upload cat images to the app.
FR3: The app shall provide near real-time breed detection from the uploaded cat images.

3.2 Training and Care
FR1: The app shall allow users to generate a personalized cat training plan.
FR2: The app shall provide personalized grooming tips.
FR3: The app shall allow users to generate a personalized exercise plan.
FR4: The app shall assist users to follow and track training and exercise plans.
FR5: The app shall provide specialized guidance regarding newborn cats, pregnant cats, and neutering.

3.3 Health and Nutrition
FR1: The app shall allow users to generate personalized nutrition plans.
FR2: The app shall allow users to track their cat’s daily nutrition intake.
FR3: The app shall allow users to input their cat’s health issues and symptoms.
FR4: The app shall provide symptom analysis reports and further guidance.
FR5: The app shall provide nearest veterinarian clinic recommendations.
FR6: The app shall provide developer contact info for veterinarians for potential sponsorships.

3.4 Accessories
FR1: The app shall allow users to choose from different categories of accessories.
FR2: The app shall provide users with a significant number of products and listings.
FR3: The app shall allow users to set a price filter for recommendations.
FR4: The app shall provide developer contact info for sellers for potential sponsorships.

3.5 Cat Bot
FR1: The app shall allow users to ask cat-care-related questions to an inbuilt chatbot.
FR2: The app shall provide short and reliable answers.

3.6 GPS Tracker
FR1: The app shall allow users to order a custom cat collar.
FR2: The app shall allow users to configure the custom collar with the app.
FR3: The app shall provide real-time GPS location of the cat collar.

4. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Reliability
NFR1: The app shall have a Mean Time Between Failures (MTBF) of at least 500 hours.
NFR2: User data shall be backed up on the back-end database to avoid data loss.

4.2 Usability
NFR3: The app shall provide a simple and straightforward user interface.
NFR4: The app shall use input validation in the chatbot to ensure cat-related questions are catered.
NFR5: The system shall allow users to complete primary functions (e.g., nutrition logging) within 3 clicks or less.

4.3 Performance
NFR6: The app shall identify the breed of uploaded images within 10 seconds.
NFR7: The app shall have an accuracy of at least 90% for correct breed detection.
NFR8: The app shall generate personalized nutrition, training, and exercise plans within 10 seconds.
NFR9: Symptom analysis should be accurate to at least 90%.
NFR10: The GPS tracker location should be accurate to within at least 15 meters.
NFR11: The app shall be able to handle at least 500 concurrent users initially.

4.4 Security
NFR12: The system shall manage user accounts with login IDs and passwords.
NFR13: The login and sign-up system shall be resistant to common vulnerabilities such as SQL injections.
