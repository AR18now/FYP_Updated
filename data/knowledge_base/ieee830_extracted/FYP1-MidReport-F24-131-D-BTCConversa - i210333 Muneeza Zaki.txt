Abstract
BTC Conversa: Generative AI Banking Chatbot is designed to simplify and automate
mundane banking through the use of conversational AI. In this Phase One for the project,
most of the concentration will be placed on the Information Bot, which greeting re-
sponses associated with account-related inquiries, balance inquiries, account statements,
and branch information. The system is developed on Flask for API management and
SQLite to simulate banking database management. It processes multiple queries via
RASA for seamlessness, making the experience personalized. Also, session management
by this bot maintains consistency across devices and browsers. This report describes the
work completed during the first phase and underlines the chatbot’s potential to bring about
a sea change in customer interactions within the banking sector.

Chapter 1
Introduction
The banking sector has steadily moved towards digital platforms, enabling customers to
manage their accounts, transfer funds, and pay bills online. However, many banking
apps still require complex navigation and lack the user-friendliness needed for day-to-day
banking tasks. In today’s ever developing world, customers demand more personalized
and faster solutions to simplify their interactions with banks.
This project, BTC Conversa, is designed to tackle these needs by introducing a conver-
sational AI-based banking assistant. Using generative AI technology, this chatbot will
automate routine banking tasks, enabling users to interact with their banks seamlessly
through text-based conversations. This will allow users to complete tasks such as check-
ing balances, managing accounts, and viewing transaction history without the need to
navigate through a myriad of screens.
1.1 Problem Statement
Traditional banking systems and apps are functional but they fall short of delivering user-
friendly experiences. Customers face delays in accessing important account information
and frequently find the interface complicated to use. This leads to poor customer satis-
faction, especially when users need personalized financial advice or quick access to their
account details.
BTC Conversa aims to solve these issues by providing a single conversational AI-based
solution. With features like real-time transaction history, automated responses to common
queries, and personalized financial insights, BTC Conversa seeks to streamline banking
for the modern day customer.

1.2 Motivation
This project is motivated by the high demand for better and easier to use banking ser-
vices. In the age of digital banking adoption — requiring more personalized, real-time
interactions from AI-powered solutions is the new normality.
The inspiration behind BTC conversa comes out of the requirement to give a more natural,
human like experience to users. By incorporating conversational AI, we want to build a
product not just to make banking tasks simpler but which also provides more personalized
insights and support for an improved customer experience.
Additionally, this project is being carried out in collaboration with BluTech Consulting,
providing valuable industry insights and guidance, ensuring that the solution aligns with
real-world needs.
1.3 Scope
The scope of BTC Conversa defines a conversational AI-powered banking assistant to
ease and automate routine banking tasks through natural language. This paper focuses,
first of all, on major functionalities for the balance inquiry, account statement retrieval,
money transfer, and branch information request. One can ask about several things in a
single message, such as "What is my balance, ntn, and e-mail?" or ask for specific details
about each transaction.
The system will be integrated seamlessly with banking for total security and personal
experiences both on the web and mobile; BTC Conversa will also include session storage
across device and browser changes to make users’ data safe and consistent throughout
their banking activities. It shall also be able to provide personalized financial insights to
users based on their transaction history for better spending management and improved
financial literacy.
BTC Conversa will allow pure financial and non-financial action handling, such as bill
payments, fund transfers across accounts, actions related to adding a new payee, and
spending alert management through text or voice-based conversations. Besides that, ad-
visory services will also be provided regarding personalized tips for budgeting, saving on
taxes, and spending patterns. It will also keep the records of all branch-related informa-
tion updated within the system for facilitating the process of finding branches based on
certain criteria related to region or availability on weekends.
In the future versions, more modules will be integrated to enable this chatbot with ad-
vanced analytics and advisory functions. BTC Conversa serves both personal banking
customers and financial institutions by providing them with scalable solutions that are

filing complaints.

The analysis module helps users understand their spending and financial behavior.

2. Payment analysis for insights into regular payments and financial habits.

Description: The user asks for their account balance, and the chatbot responds with the

Description: The user requests their transaction history, and the chatbot provides a sum-

Actors: User, System
Precondition: User is authenticated and has requested a valid date range.
Flow of Events:
• The user asks, "Show me my transaction history for the last 30 days."
• The system verifies the user’s authentication.
• The system retrieves the transaction history from the database.
• The system presents the transaction details to the user.
Postcondition: The user is presented with the requested transaction history.

Chapter 3
System Overview
3.1 General Description
BTC Conversa is designed to be a conversational AI banking assistant and is envisioned
to guide the users in performing routine banking tasks. This encapsulates most of the key
functionalities, including account balance inquiries, transaction history, fund transfers,
and requests for branch information. Users can interact with the system using a chatbot
interface that processes real-time natural language input using RASA.
The architecture is client-server, where the interface of the chatbot interacts with the back-
end services and APIs to fetch and process information related to banking. The system
sustains security and usability for both web and mobile platforms by managing the ses-
sions to ensure continuity between devices.
3.2 Architectural Design
The architecture of BTC Conversa follows a modular client-server design. The primary
modules include:
1. Frontend: Provides the chatbot interface for user interactions. Users can query in-
formation such as account balance, transaction history, and transfer funds through this
interface.
2. API Layer (Flask): Serves as the intermediary between the frontend and the backend
systems. It handles API requests, authenticates users, and routes the queries to the appro-
priate service.
3. Backend Database (SQLite): Stores essential banking data such as user accounts,
transaction records, and branch information. The database is accessed via API calls.
4. Chatbot (RASA): Handles natural language processing and intent recognition. It iden-

5. Session Management: Manages user sessions to ensure secure and personalized ex-

habits, the LLaMA model is invoked to process data and generate insights.

3.4 Data Design
The data design for BTC Conversa is focused on how user data, account information,
and transaction history are stored and processed. The system uses a relational database
(SQLite) to manage the banking data.
- Users Table: Contains user profile information including name, email, and authentica-
tion credentials.
- Accounts Table: Stores account details such as account number, balance, and the type
of account (e.g., savings, current).
- Transactions Table: Logs all financial transactions made by the user, including the
transaction date, amount, and associated account.
- Branches Table: Contains information about bank branches, such as the address, con-
tact details, and availability on weekends.
Data Storage and Processing: - All user-related data is stored securely using encrypted
storage. - The backend processes incoming queries by retrieving relevant information
from the database and sends the processed data back to the frontend. - The system en-
sures secure communication through session tokens, which protect user data during inter-
actions.

Chapter 4
Conclusions and Future Work
4.1 Conclusions
BTC Conversa is a conversational AI Banking Assistant engineered to be more interactive
with the user’s banking services. The initial part of the project involves setting up the fun-
damental architecture and introducing basic features such as balance inquiry, transaction
history, and elementary fund transfers.
During this stage, the chatbot is at a partial implementation using RASA for natural lan-
guage understanding and SQLite to simulate banking databases. It can process simple
queries and respond in real time. Advanced functionality remains under development.
The project is in development with BluTech Consulting. The first version has created
the bedrock, while further versions are needed to get close to the envisioned intelligent
assistant for full assistance in banking. Initial progress is promising, and the modular
architecture of the system ensures that future features can be integrated seamlessly.
Future stages of the project will be directed at improving overall functionality, usability,
and security features of BTC Conversa. As this project progresses with development, it
shall eventually become a fully capable and secure banking assistant that should appeal
to the increasing demands of this modern banking industry.
