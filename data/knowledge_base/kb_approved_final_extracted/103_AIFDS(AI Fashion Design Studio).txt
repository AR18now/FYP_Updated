Project: AI Fashion Design Studio - AI-Powered Fashion Design and Marketing Platform

1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This document outlines the objectives, features, and overall vision for the AI Fashion Design Studio, a system that uses generative AI to expedite and enhance the fashion design process.

1.2 Scope
The platform converts sketches to digital images, creates 3D models, enables virtual try-on, generates new designs via a crossover mechanism, and automatically produces marketing materials (image and video ads). It focuses on adult clothing.

1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, Abbreviations
- AI: Artificial Intelligence
- GUI: Graphical User Interface
- API: Application Programming Interface
- 3D: Three-Dimensional

2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
2.1 Product Perspective
The AI Fashion Design Studio aims to revolutionize the fashion industry by streamlining the long development cycle and enabling rapid experimentation and customization through AI.

2.2 Product Functions (Summary)
- Template Management.
- Sketch to Image Conversion (using Stable Diffusion).
- 3D Modeling and Visualization.
- Virtual Try-On.
- Crossover Design Mechanism.
- Marketing Material Generation (image and video ads).

2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
- Fashion Designers: Primary users who use the system to improve and optimize the design process.
- E-commerce Retailers: Use the platform to create online catalogs and provide virtual try-on experiences.
- Customers: Benefit from personalized shopping experiences via virtual try-on and custom design.

2.4 Operating Environment
Web application.

3. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Template Management
FR1.1: Users shall be able to browse and select from a provided list of fashion templates.
FR1.2: The system shall display the selected template, ready for further customization.

3.2 Sketch to Image Conversion
FR2.1: The system shall convert hand-drawn sketches into detailed 2D digital images using a fine-tuned Stable Diffusion model.
FR2.2: High-resolution digital images that closely resemble the original sketches shall be produced.

3.3 3D Modeling and Visualization
FR3.1: The system shall transform 2D digital images into 3D models that are rotatable and viewable from any angle.
FR3.2: A fully interactive 3D model of the clothing item shall be available for detailed inspection.

3.4 Virtual Try-On
FR4.1: The system shall simulate how clothing items look on virtual avatars using a VITON model.
FR4.2: A visualization of the garment as worn by the avatar shall be provided, offering realistic representations of fit and style.

3.5 Crossover Design Mechanism
FR5.1: The system shall allow for the fusion of elements from two different garments to create a new, unique design.
FR5.2: A new garment design that incorporates the selected elements from the original garments shall be produced.

3.6 Clothing Image Ads
FR6.1: The system shall automatically generate multiple marketing images featuring models wearing the designed clothes in various settings.
FR6.2: Professionally styled images suitable for marketing purposes shall be displayed.

3.7 Video Ads
FR7.1: The system shall create short video clips showcasing the clothes in dynamic custom scenes.
FR7.2: A 5-10 second marketing video that highlights the garment features dynamically shall be produced.

4. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Reliability
NFR1: The system shall have an MTBF of no less than 100 hours.
NFR2: The system will implement regular data backups, automated recovery processes, and fail-over mechanisms.

4.2 Usability
NFR3: The system shall allow users to complete common design tasks within a few steps.
NFR4: The system shall comply with web accessibility standards.

4.3 Performance
NFR5: The system shall generate high-resolution digital images from user-uploaded sketches within a minute.
NFR6: 3D models from 2D digital images shall be rendered within a minute.
NFR7: Virtual try-on simulations shall generate results within 50 seconds of input.
NFR8: Marketing Content Generation module will generate content within 3-4 minutes.
NFR9: The system must handle up to 300 concurrent users without noticeable degradation.
NFR10: Uploading and processing images under 50MB shall happen in less than a minute.

4.4 Security
NFR11: The system must implement robust authentication and authorization mechanisms to ensure only registered users can access the design studio.
NFR12: The system shall comply with GDPR, CCPA, and other relevant data protection regulations.
NFR13: Compliance with copyright and intellectual property laws must be ensured.
