2.2.2 Image Generation 
To solve our image generation task, we test multiple models to get cartoon characters 
according to our use case. Some models miserably failed to generate image with a high 
quality(greater than 1080x720p), while some models are giving noise in output. Here are 
some models we test: 
1. Runwayml/stable-diffusion-v1-5: Output of v1-5 optimised around 512x512 
pixels, that’s the major issue we’re having with it. 
2. Stabilityai/stable-diffusion-2-1: Results become so messed up and high contrasted, 
mostly cartoonized in a bad way 
3. Playgroundai/playground-v2.5 
Finally we’ve decide playgroundai version 2.5 ust because of it’s particular focus on 
enhanced color and contrast, improved generation for multi-aspect ratios, and improved 
human-centric fine details. 
2.2.3 Convert Image to SVG 
To make image in an editable form we will convert our image into svg format using 
using tracing and contouring techniques. Here are some of the techniques we tried in this 
module and some issues faced: 
• Loss of color information: Tracing typically converts the image to grayscale or 
binary, losing all color information from the original RGB image. This results in a 
monochrome output that doesn’t capture the richness of the original image. 
• Edge detection limitation: Tracing relies heavily on edge detection, by 
differentiating between colors inside images. But when similar contrast were 
found tracing could not make a boundary

Conclusion 
 
In conclusion, the Story Animator project will enable reversing the problems animators, 
designers, and other artists encounter when designing and modifying their animated 
tales. From a unique set of features, the platform simplifies character design through the 
use of image-to-SVG converter, background remover, GIF maker, and an AI character 
creator. In addition to saving time and money, these features promote innovation and 
creativity among a range of user classes, including content producers and professional 
animators. Scalability, security, and a seamless user experience are guaranteed by the 
strong system architecture, which also features a user-friendly interface and dependable

design fields by providing high-quality tools to a wider audience, facilitating easier 
access to animation, and affordability.
