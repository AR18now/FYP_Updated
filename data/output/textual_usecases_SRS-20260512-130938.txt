Use Case: FR-01 - Search Books
Primary Actor: Reader
Stakeholders: Reader, Librarian, Administrator
Preconditions: Reader has logged into the system.
Main Success Scenario:
1. Reader enters search terms into the search bar.
2. System queries the database based on the entered criteria.
3. Results list displays matching titles along with additional details.
Extensions:
- If no results match the query, display a message informing the reader.
Postcondition: Reader receives a list of potential book matches or confirmation of no matches found.
Trace to FR Id: FR-01

Use Case: FR-02 - Borrow Books
Primary Actor: Reader
Stakeholders: Reader, Librarian, Administrator
Preconditions: Reader has selected a book to borrow and meets membership rules.
Main Success Scenario:
1. Reader selects a book and initiates the borrowing process.
2. System checks reader eligibility and loan history.
3. Loan record is updated accordingly.
4. Reader receives a confirmation message.
Extensions:
- If the reader exceeds their borrowing limit, deny the request and inform them.
Postcondition: Reader either successfully borrows a book or receives feedback on why they cannot.
Trace to FR Id: FR-02
