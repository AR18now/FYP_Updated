1.1.3 Vulnerability to Adversarial Environmental Conditions 
 
Traditional systems operate under optimum environmental conditions, including 
adequate lighting conditions and unobstructed field of vision. However, with variations 
in environ-mental conditions, their performance is significantly impacted.

working of the system leading towards decreased or ineffective surveillance in critical 
situations.

Current systems have high energy consumption. CCTV cameras and their control unit 
needs to operational 24/7 to provide surveillance, resulting in high energy 
consumption and costs. 
 
 
 
1.1.6 No Support for Law Enforcement or Emergency Services 
 
Existing systems have no integrated functionalities to alert emergency or law 
enforcement services in real time during security incidents, resulting in delayed 
response.

2.3.1 Reliability 
 
The system shall maintain a reliable architecture with a 99.9pc up -time. The system 
due to its nature shall provide surveillance continuously regardless of environmental 
conditions. It shall be robust to handle any unexpected failures or errors. 
 
2.3.2 Scalability 
 
The system shall be scalable to monitor larger spaces upto the transmission range of 
the Wi-Fi signals. The system shall provide with integration of multiple Channel State 
 
Information (CSI) IoT recorders to cover more larger areas and provide a better 
accuracy. 
 
2.3.3 Usability 
 
The system shall provide with usable and interactive web and mobile applications to 
per-form related tasks. It shall provide a visual representation of monitored data. The 
system shall be simple to use and require minimum technical expertise to operate. The 
mobile app shall have support for offline usage for continuous monitoring. 
 
 
2.3.4 Performance 
 
The system shall meet real -time requirements for monitoring. It shall record Channel 
State Information (CSI) values at a regular interval to enable for real time monitoring. 
The system shall provide minimal latency to provide near instant feedback to the 
users. The model shall have a simple input to process data and predict the pattern, 
indicating presence of humans or objects, in the data to provide feedback to the user. 
 
 
2.3.5 Maintainability 
 
The system shall be developed using industry line development techniques to provide 
with a modular architecture. The system shall be documented properly to provide with 
easy to maintain code bases across all modules. The applications shall be containerized 
to maintain them.

2.3.6 Availability 
 
The system shall be available all the time with maximum uptime. The Flask API, web 
and mobile applications shall be deployed on cloud to ensure system operationality. 
 
2.3.7 Security 
 
The system shall employ industry line security to provide with secure and easy to use. 
User authentication shall utilize JsonWebTokens (JWT) for secure login. The system 
shall leverage Role -Based Access Control to only allow authorized users to view and 
manipulate surveillance data. 
 
2.3.8 Interoperability 
 
The system shall support integration with external systems like emergency services for 
alerts during security incident. The system shall allow for use cases beyond 
surveillance like telecommunications optimization, etc.
