2.3 Non-Functional Requirements
2.3.1 Performance
• The response time of functions should be at most 2 seconds.
• The system should be able to handle at least 1000 concurent users.
2.3.2 Security
• The system should encrypt user passwords using a salted hash algorithm (e.g.,
bcrypt) with a minimum of 10 characters.
• The system should implement rate limiting on login attempts to prevent brute-force
attacks (e.g., 5 attempts within 10 minutes).
• The system should implement role-based access control (RBAC) to restrict access
to administrative functions.
2.3.3 Usability
• The system should provide clear and concise error messages for invalid user input.
2.3.4 Data Integrity
• The system should maintain a minimum of 99.5% data accuracy for product and
user information (e.g., product details, images, categories, brands).
• The system should update product and user information in real-time to reflect changes
in inventory or product details.
2.3.5 Accuracy
• The system should achieve a minimum of 80% accuracy in recommending products
based on user preferences and behavior.
• The system should continuously learn and improve recommendation accuracy through
machine learning algorithms.

Chapter 3
System Overview
The proposed online clothing system is the prototype for a centralized platform to fulfill
our vision of a centralised shopping system containing the items of all the brands in
Pakistan that want to sell their items online. The platform will offer a curated selection
tailored to individual preferences through recommendation algorithms. Users will be
able to easily browse, compare and purchase items, mainly various styles of shalwar,
kamez, pants, and shirts. To enhance the shopping experience, a chatbot will be
integrated to provide assistance and answer user queries. The platform will also prioritize
data privacy and security, implementing robust measures to protect user information.
3.1 Architectural Design
MeriCloset is a personalized shopping app designed to enhance the online shopping
experience for users by offering tailored recommendations and features. The app aims to
bridge the gap between user preferences and the vast array of clothing options available
online. By utilizing advanced data insights and machine learning, MeriCloset will
provide users with a curated shopping experience that considers their individual tastes,
body measurements, and occasions for which they are shopping.

3.3 Data Design
In this project, we are working locally so everything is within a local environment. We
have created a database that we have built on MongoDB as our data is very versatile due
to the nature of our project. The integration of this with our application and its future
deployment is also something we are adept at hence our choice.
We fetch the item details from our database to our application, the images are stored in
link format and user can view the items images and the relevant details. This is
something we have done from the beginning in order to not deal with the hassle of
creating a file system for the purpose of documenting our progress.

Chapter 4
Conclusions and Future Work
We have created an application that works as a platform which unites and brings together
Pakistan diverse fashion offerings. This is our proposed vision for a centralized platform
which will provide consumers with a seamless, efficient and aggregated shopping
experience. Our application provides a tailored selection for individual preferences
through the use of recommendation applications. Our users can freely express their
fashion sense and share it with others through lookbooks. They can store their favorite
products in their personal online closets.
Our project, however, only serves as a prototype for a platform to provide greater reach
for domestic brands. Some items have been taken from public datasets while others have
been scrapped from publicly available web pages of local brands. Future application can
be created can use this project as a jumping off point to reach greater heights. It is our
hope that these applications will provide Pakistan-made clothing a chance to truly
flourish.
