Chapter 1 
1. Introduction 
The purpose of this project document is to outline a detailed plan for the development 
of an interactive virtual reality (VR) application that will transform video drone -
footages of residential housing society into a 3D model for a virtual tour. The 
document will outline the project's problem we are going to address, what motivat ed 
us, scope of our project, deliverables, and all the technical processes involved in 
bringing an innovative solution to overcome the problem which we are going to 
implement in our final year project. The detailed overview of the project would mainly 
focus on creating a virtual environment of residential housing societies. This is a VR 
based application, which will provide a platform for stakeholders such as real estate 
builders associated with the society or society management to give a virtual tour of the 
society environment to the customers who are interested in investing in the society. A 
web application will be provided to the stakeholders, where they can upload video 
drone footage which will be used to generate a 3D model of the environment. The 3D 
model will be accessible through a VR based application using VR headset to virtually 
tour the environment. This project will help bridge the gap in the industry by providing 
an innovative solution to visualize and explore residential societies using modern-day 
technologies.

2. Project Vision 
2.1 Problem Statement 
The real estate market in Pakistan is growing at an unprecedented pace, with investors 
seeking safer, more Secure investments. Yet despite its rapid expansion, the industry 
still relies heavily on Traditional methods of Property visualization —photos, 2D ma ps, 
and occasional video tours. In traditional methods of exploring and assessing a new 
Residential society, individuals must physically visit each street and corner to get a 
comprehensive understanding of the area. This process is not only Time-consuming and 
physically demanding, but also impractical for those with Mobility limitations or busy 
schedules. The existing approaches to remotely visualize residential properties and 
societies, for instance, 'Google Street View and static 2D maps,' are inadequate to 
provide the required flexibility and interactivity for detailed exploration. Such models do 
not get updated in real -time and are very expensive in their development and 
maintenance; hence, not sufficient for the Customer, real estate developers, and 
investors who base their decisions on engaging, instructive visualizations. This indeed 
calls for a more efficient, interactive way of turning Drone footage into highly detailed, 
immersive 3D models. Increasingly, TerraVerse 3D meets this challenge with Virtu al 
Reality and AI-driven technology to power the generation of 3D models from drone video 
footage. The system has been speeding up the whole process more economically and 
much more attractively.

institutes can use TerraVerse 3D to depict several real examples related to town

properties, international investors can make more informed decisions about 
purchasing properties abroad. This can reduce the risk associated with investing 
in unfamiliar markets.

2.4 Project Scope 
1. Data Collection and Preprocessing 
● Stabilizing and Preprocessing the Video Footage: 
o Video stabilization software will be used to remove unwanted movements 
and jitters from footage. 
o Footage will be preprocessed by correcting lens distortions, adjusting 
lighting, and image quality will be enhanced for better 3D reconstruction. 
2. 3D Model Reconstruction 
● Neural Radiance Fields (NeRF): 
o NeRF: We will use neural networks to illustrate the model in 3D 
environment, allowing for highly detailed and realistic 3D reconstructions. 
● Optimizing the 3D Model for VR Performance: 
o Simplifying the 3D model by reducing the polygon count while important 
details are being preserved. 
o Optimizing lighting and applying texture maps so that to enhance realism 
without compromising performance. 
o Ensuring that the model meets VR performance standards and providing a 
smooth and immersive experience. 
3. VR Application Development 
● Building the VR Environment in Unity: 
o The 3d model will be imported into unity 
o Setting the VR scene with lighting, textures, and interactive elements. 
● Implementing Smooth Navigation Controls: 
o Developing navigation controls for users so that they can move around the VR 
environment. 
o Compatibility with Oculus Rift will be ensured including mappings and input 
handling.

 2.4 Constraints 
Technical Constraints: 
● Hardware Limitations : The VR application requires users to have access to 
compatible VR headsets (e.g., Oculus Rift) and powerful enough hardware to run 
the application smoothly. 
User Constraints: 
● Accessibility: Ensuring the VR application is easy-to-use and accessible to 
everyone with varying levels of technical expertise and physical abilities. 
Scalability Constraints: 
● Multiple Societies: Initially, the project will focus on a single residential society, 
and expanding to multiple societies will require significant additional resources 
and planning. We will be covering 500 kanal of landmark. 
Real-time 3D Reconstruction: 
• Definition: The whole project is based only on pre-recorded footage from drones 
to create the 3D model of the residential society. Real-time video processing and 
reconstruction is not implemented. 
• Explanation: This limitation allows the project to focus on the accuracy and 
quality of the 3D models created from captured footage, instead of developing a 
complex system for real-time processing. By relying on pre-recorded footage, the 
project will create a more controlled environment for model generation, resulting 
in better overall quality. However, it constrains the application's ability to react to 
dynamic changes within the environment.

Multi-Society Implementation: 
We will be considering only one residential society for the initial phase of the project and 
will expand to multiple societies in the pipeline for upcoming phases. 
Extensive AI Training: 
We will train NLP to provide information related to the specific residential society only, 
using the data that is shared by that society. 
Model Updates:

or revisions over time.

3. Software Requirement Specifications 
3.1 List of features 
● 3D Model Generation 
● Interactive Virtual Reality Experience 
● User-Friendly Web Application 
● AI-Powered Q/A Information System 
● Detailed Property Visualization 
3.2. Functional Requirement 
• The system should provide utilities to stabilize and pre -process the captured 
video output. 
• It shall generate an accurate 3D model output from the uploaded drone footage. 
• The system shall support the creation of a VR environment using Oculus. 
• It shall be able to share related video drone footage with users via an intuitive web 
interface. 
• The AI model can process user queries regarding the residential society and 
provide relevant answers, based on the data the model will receive. 
• VR interaction shall include selecting points of interest and accessing further 
information on specific areas or amenities within the society. 
• It shall ensure optimization in performance for 3D models in VR, with high 
navigability and low latency. 
 
 
Page 15 of 36 
 
3.3. Quality Attributes 
Availability: 
• The system will have to reach a minimum of 99.99% uptime to ensure that the web 
application and VR features are always up and accessible for users and 
stakeholders alike. 
Reliability: 
• The system shall ensure that the latency in authenticating a user and establishing 
a network session is below 50 milliseconds to present a consistent response. 
Usability: 
• The interface shall be intuitive and self -descriptive so that users can easily learn 
how to move in the VR space and understand the application features with 
minimal training. 
3.4. NON-FUNCTIONAL Requirements 
 Compatibility: 
• Besides being compatible with major browsers like Chrome, Firefox, it should 
also be optimized to work on Edge, or desktop. 
Performance: 
• The system shall process uploaded drone footage and generate the 3D model 
within a maximum time of 30 minutes for a typical residential society (up to 500 
kanal). 
• The VR application shall maintain a frame rate of at least 60 frames per second 
(FPS) to ensure smooth navigation and an immersive experience. 
User Support: 
● The system shall provide a help and support feature, including FAQs, tutorials, 
and a ticketing system for user inquiries.
