1.2 Scope
Our project aims to transform how we consume information from the internet into some-
thing quicker, more engaging, and more informative. We want to create a dynamic project
that gathers information from articles, websites, and other platforms, converting it all into
a single summary that is to the point. This summary will then be converted from text to
speech, and that will be done using an open source model, which will support both English
and Urdu. Moreover, our custom AI anchor will deliver the news, which we will train on
our own dataset using the First Order Motion Model (FOMM). Our project’s main aim is
to keep the content we gather concise so it is easier for our users to stay up to date with
the information they require with them needing to read lengthy articles or websites. The
videos are set to have a length between 30 to 120 seconds, as we feel this is the perfect
duration for keeping our users interested while still conveying all the main points.

Chapter 3
System Overview
FUNCTIONALITY:
InfoMorph is a web-based platform that automates the conversion of any article or blog
post into a concise and easily digestible summary, which is then narrated by an AI-
generated video anchor. Users can either input a URL, and the platform scrapes the
content, or they can input a topic, and the platform will provide a list of relevant URLs
for the user to choose from. The selected content is then processed using natural language
processing (NLP) to categorize and summarize the information. The summarized text is
transformed into speech, and an AI video anchor narrates the content in a video format.
These videos are designed to be short, engaging, and suitable for social media platforms
like Instagram, making it easy for users to consume information quickly and efficiently.
CONTEXT: infoMorph addresses the broader issue of information overload and the grow-
ing preference for visual and summarized content especially among younger generations.
Instead of focusing solely on news, the platform allows users to interact with a wide range
of articles and blogs. With the shift towards digital consumption, many people find it chal-
lenging to sift through lengthy content, and InfoMorph provides a solution by presenting
information in a simplified, engaging, and visually appealing way. This makes it suitable
for anyone who wants to save time while staying informed, whether about current events,
lifestyle, or any other subject.
DESIGN: InfoMorph is designed with simplicity and efficiency in mind. The platform
uses web scraping tools to gather text from any URL, and NLP techniques categorize the
content. Open-source large language models (LLMs) are employed to ensure the sum-
maries are concise and meaningful. The summarized text is converted into speech using
advanced text-to-speech technology, and a deepfake AI anchor narrates the content in a
video format. The design ensures the final product is optimized for social media plat-
forms, particularly for reels and short videos, ensuring the content is visually appealing
and easily shareable.
