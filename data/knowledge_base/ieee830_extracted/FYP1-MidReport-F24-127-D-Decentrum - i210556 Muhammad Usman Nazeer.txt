System Overview
It is a blockchain-based social media system that enhances the interaction of users while
safeguarding the data confidentiality and ownership rights over the contents. It allows
users to create, share, and sell the content in a decentralized manner.
The platform benefits from concerns regarding the private data handling of traditional
social media and generates revenue without any intermediaries by permitting the users to
monetize their contents, in a community-driven manner.
3.1 Architectural Design
3.1.1 Modular Program Structure
The system adopts modular architecture with components that are independently func-
tional but maintain interrelationship. It ensures scalability and maintainability and inte-
grability of blockchain functionalities.
3.1.1.1 Content Management
• Functionality: Allow users to generate content and share it while giving ownership
right through blockchain.
• Relationship: Coordinates with Profile Management in linking up the content to
the user profiles and identity validation for permission management.

3.3 Data Design
3.3.1 Key Entities
• Users: The basic information such as ID,name,balance will be placed in this table.
Password will be hashed and the content on profile will be stored on IPFS as storage
is decentralized.
• Posts: Posts are the key aspect in content creation. The posts made by users such
as videos,text will be stored on IPFS, with all required fields.
• Reactions: The given by users are key aspect for interaction between users. They
can be stored on chain with reference to user and post on the blockchain.
• Message: Chats between users can be encrypted and stored on IPFS, with fields
such as sender,receiver etc.. on the blockchain.
• Groups: Decentralized management of group and community membership, which
includes group identifiers and users, allows tracking of membership on-chain with
the possibility of storing off-chain group data, such as topics, on IPFS.
• Advertisements: Ad’s that are posted can be referenced with tokens and store their
content on IPFS.

3.3 Data Design
• Report: User’s can report content or users and this information can be stored on
IPFS along with moderators who evaluate the post.
• Monetization: The request for monetization can be made by users to request for
tokens rewarded and are also stored with approval or rejection by smart contracts
on the blockchain.
• Share: A record of users posting will be kept on-chain and the original content will
be stored on IPFS.
3.3.2 Data Storage Items
3.3.2.1 IPFS
The major content that includes videos, text, images, etc.. will be stored using IPFS
mechanism. Whenever a content is posted by user it is pinned by IPFS and in the database
a CID is referenced.
3.3.2.2 Smart Contracts
In this blockchain-based application, smart contracts will manage various functionali-
ties: user accounts for balance tracking, profile changes, and password recovery; content
management for post creation, sharing, and reactions, with metadata stored on-chain and
content referenced on IPFS; monetization requests for validating and distributing token
rewards; group management for adding or removing users from communities; and ad
management for overseeing ad tokens and tracking interactions with advertisements.
3.3.2.3 Blockchain Integration
Every action, including posting content, reacting, or sending messages, is written to
the blockchain and provides absolute transparency and immutability. This leaves the
blockchain free to manage tokens, user balances, and monetization rewards, while IPFS
manages all the actual data.

Conclusions and Future Work
In conclusions, the development a decentralized social media platform is make a step
towards a revolutionize the way of interaction between people. By leveraging blockchain
technology, it will provide users a decentralized platform where users can express their
thoughts freely without fear of censoring their content by a third party or centralized
authority. This will encourage users to take ownership of the content that is shared by
them and they will also be able to earn rewards through monetizing their content. Their
data will be protected and will not be breached by other people. Our goal is to create a
environment for users where their right to freedom of speech is protected and they can
express their feelings in front of others.
