Project: RastaSaaz - AI-Driven Resume Refactoring and Job Application Automation

1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This document describes RastaSaaz, an automatic resume refactoring system designed to boost job placement by optimizing resumes for specific job requirements and automating the job application process.

1.2 Scope
The system will parse resumes, match skills with job descriptions, rank resumes, refactor high-ranking resumes to highlight relevant content, and automate the application process on LinkedIn.

1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, Abbreviations
- ATS: Applicant Tracking System
- AI: Artificial Intelligence
- API: Application Programming Interface
- PDF: Portable Document Format

2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
2.1 Product Perspective
RastaSaaz fills the gap in existing software by automatically refactoring resumes based on job descriptions, prioritizing relevant sections and removing irrelevant details to increase success in the job market.

2.2 Product Functions (Summary)
- Data collection and resume parsing.
- Job post scraping (LinkedIn) and ranking.
- Resume refactoring and regeneration.
- Automation of job application (via Selenium).

2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
- Job Seekers: Individuals looking to maximize their chances of getting a job.

2.4 Operating Environment
Web-based application.

3. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Data Collection and Resume Parsing
FR1.1: The system shall allow users to upload resumes in PDF format.
FR1.2: The system shall convert the resume from PDF to JSON format.
FR1.3: The system shall extract and categorize key details (education, experience, skills).
FR1.4: The system shall store extracted details in a structured database.

3.2 Job Posts Scraping and Ranking
FR2.1: The system shall scrape IT-related jobs from LinkedIn based on filters.
FR2.2: The system shall extract required skills and qualifications from job descriptions.
FR2.3: The system shall compare resume details with job descriptions and assign a rank.
FR2.4: The system shall analyze the resume and match with job description to identify skill gaps.
FR2.5: The system shall store job requirements in a structured database.

3.3 Refactoring of Resume
FR3.1: The system shall prioritize relevant skills, projects, and experience based on job descriptions.
FR3.2: The system shall automatically rearrange resume sections based on ATS standards.
FR3.3: The system shall generate ATS-compliant resumes tailored to different job descriptions.
FR3.4: The system shall allow users to download the resume in PDF.

3.4 Automation of Job Application
FR4.1: The system shall automate the job application process for relevant job postings on LinkedIn using Selenium.
FR4.2: The system shall extract data from the resume to auto-fill job applications.
FR4.3: The system shall be tested for automation and fully functional at launch.

4. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Reliability
NFR1: The system shall have an MTBF of at least 400 hours.
NFR2: Upon failure, the system shall maintain user data with no data loss.

4.2 Usability
NFR3: The system shall provide an intuitive dashboard.
NFR4: Error messages shall be clear and concise with suggestions.
NFR5: The system shall meet accessibility standards.

4.3 Performance
NFR6: The system shall parse and analyze a resume within 15 seconds of upload.
NFR7: Job description parsing and resume matching shall complete within 10 seconds per job.

4.4 Security
NFR8: All sensitive user data shall be protected against unauthorized access.
NFR9: Field-level encryption in MongoDB shall be used for sensitive data.
