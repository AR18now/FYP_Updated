**IEEE 830-1998 SRS for LMS**

**1. INTRODUCTION**
- **Purpose:** To define the system requirements for a Learning Management System (LMS) that enables instructors to manage courses, quizzes, and student progress while providing learners with tools to engage in coursework and receive certifications.
- **Scope:** This document outlines the functional and non-functional requirements of the LMS.
- **Definitions/Acronyms:**
  - **LMS:** Learning Management System
  - **FR:** Functional Requirement
  - **NFR:** Non-functional Requirement
- **References:** IEEE 830-1998, ISO/IEC 25010:2011
- **Overview:** The LMS will facilitate online education by enabling instructors to create and manage courses and assessments, and allowing learners to participate in these activities and receive certificates upon completion.

**2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION**
- **Product Perspective:** The LMS is designed for educational institutions and individual educators to deliver online courses and manage student performance.
- **Product Functions:**
  - Instructors can create courses, upload videos, add quizzes, and set grading rules.
  - Learners can enroll, track progress, take timed quizzes, and download certificates.
  - The system supports cohorts, discussion threads, announcements, and basic plagiarism checks for assignments.
  - It handles assignment deadlines, late submissions with penalties, and allows instructors to export grades and feedback.
- **User Characteristics:**
  - Instructors: Educators with administrative access to create and manage courses.
  - Learners: Students who enroll in courses and complete coursework.
- **Constraints:**
  - The system must be accessible on various devices and browsers.
  - Data privacy and security must be maintained.
- **Assumptions/Dependencies:**
  - Internet connectivity is required for all users.
  - Users have appropriate credentials to access the system.

**3. SPECIFIC REQUIREMENTS**

**3.1 External Interface Requirements**
- **User Interface:**
  - Instructors: Web interface for course creation, quiz management, and grade export.
  - Learners: Web interface for course enrollment, progress tracking, and certificate download.
- **Hardware:**
  - Compatible with standard desktop and laptop computers.
  - Mobile device compatibility for learners.
- **Software:**
  - Compatible with major web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge).
- **Communication:**
  - Secure communication channels for user authentication and data transmission.

**3.2 Functional Requirements**
- **FR-1: Course Creation**
  - Instructors can create new courses.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-2: Video Upload**
  - Instructors can upload video content to courses.
  - Priority: Medium
- **FR-3: Quiz Addition**
  - Instructors can add quizzes to courses.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-4: Grading Rules**
  - Instructors can set grading rules for quizzes and assignments.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-5: Learner Enrollment**
  - Learners can enroll in courses.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-6: Progress Tracking**
  - Learners can track their progress in courses.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-7: Timed Quizzes**
  - Learners can take timed quizzes.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-8: Certificate Download**
  - Learners can download certificates upon course completion.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-9: Cohorts**
  - The system supports cohort management.
  - Priority: Medium
- **FR-10: Discussion Threads**
  - The system supports discussion threads within courses.
  - Priority: Medium
- **FR-11: Announcements**
  - The system supports course announcements.
  - Priority: Medium
- **FR-12: Plagiarism Checks**
  - The system performs basic plagiarism checks for assignments.
  - Priority: Low
- **FR-13: Assignment Deadlines**
  - The system enforces assignment deadlines.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-14: Late Submissions**
  - The system applies penalties for late submissions.
  - Priority: High
- **FR-15: Grade Export**
  - Instructors can export grades and feedback.
  - Priority: High

**3.3 Non-functional Requirements**
- **Performance:**
  - The system must handle at least 100 concurrent users without significant degradation in performance.
- **Security:**
  - User data must be encrypted both in transit and at rest.
  - The system must comply with GDPR and other relevant data protection regulations.
- **Reliability:**
  - The system must be available 99.9% of the time.
- **Availability:**
  - The system must be accessible 24/7.
- **Usability:**
  - The system must be intuitive and easy to use for both instructors and learners.
- **Maintainability:**
  - The system must be easily upgradable and modifiable.
- **Portability:**
  - The system must be deployable on multiple platforms and operating systems.
