1.2 Scope
Salon360 is a web application that will combine virtual hair try-ons with a salon management
system to create an efficient and unified experience for salons and their clients. It will include
management tools that will handle salon operations such as booking appointments, and main-
taining employee and customer records with POS integration.
The user module would provide three options to clients. The recommendation and try-on fea-
tures will require clients to upload a photo of themselves and get recommendations based on
face shape or try-on hairstyles and view realistic outputs. The virtual hair color try-on will
range from basic to bold colors. The booking option is an integration into the management
module.
To maintain the quality of our virtual try-on, the system will handle only front-facing 2D images
with the hair and face completely visible. Any obstructions that cover the head like hats or cover
the face like face masks will not be catered and the user would have to re-upload an image that
follows the guidelines provided. The image must also be from an appropriate distance (similar
to a passport photo but with the entire hair visible) and must be taken in ample light. The
output would be given on the image the user uploaded, and no other angles would be provided.
Additionally, our scope is limited to performing try-ons on static images only; real-time videos
will not be supported.
