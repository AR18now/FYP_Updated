SOFTWARE REQUIREMENTS SPECIFICATION (IEEE 830)
Project: Decentrum - Decentralized Social Media Platform
================================================================================

1. INTRODUCTION
1.1 Purpose
This document describes the requirements for Decentrum, a decentralized social media platform leveraging blockchain technology to provide users with control over their data, enhanced privacy, and freedom from centralized authority censorship.

1.2 Scope
The platform enables users to engage, communicate, and share content freely without third-party intervention. Key features include content creation/sharing/reaction, profile management with privacy controls, free content monetization, reward system (tokens for watching ads), community/group creation, private messaging, and KYC implementation to prevent bots.

1.3 Definitions, Acronyms, Abbreviations
- KYC: Know Your Customer
- IPFS: InterPlanetary File System
- Blockchain: Distributed ledger technology

2. OVERALL DESCRIPTION
2.1 Product Perspective
Decentrum addresses concerns about data privacy, censorship, and lack of user control inherent in centralized social media networks by using peer-to-peer and blockchain technologies.

2.2 Product Functions (Summary)
- Content Management: Creation, sharing, reaction (likes/comments).
- Profile Management: Personal info editing, account & privacy controls.
- Monetization and Rewards: Free content monetization, token rewards for viewing ads.
- Community Management & Interactions: Group formation, community forums, private messaging.

2.3 User Classes and Characteristics
- General Users: Majority who interact with content, manage profiles, engage with others.
- Content Creators: Focus on producing original content (videos, images, text) and monetizing it.
- Community Builders: Create/manage groups and communities, facilitate discussions.
- Privacy Conscious Users: Prioritize security and control over communications and data visibility.
- Reward Seekers: Motivated by the token-based reward system, engage by watching ads.
- Moderators: Maintain platform safety, review reported content.

3. FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
3.1 Content Management
FR1: The system shall allow content creation and posting in various forms (text, video, images, links to articles/blogs).
FR2: The system shall allow users to react to content through likes or comments.
FR3: The system shall enable users to share content posted on the platform with family and friends.
FR4: The system shall allow users to tag their friends on any post.
FR5: The system shall send reported content to 13 random users to verify if it is explicit.

3.2 Profile Management
FR1: The system shall allow users to make changes to personal information (profile/cover photos).
FR2: The system shall enable users to manage and customize their timeline.
FR3: The system shall allow users to change their passwords.
FR4: The system shall enable users to manage post visibility settings.
FR5: The system shall enable users to customize notification preferences.
FR6: The system shall implement KYC mechanism to cater to bots.

3.3 Monetization and Rewards
FR1: The system shall enable content creators to monetize their content without requiring any premium version.
FR2: Upon registration, the system shall give a reward token to new users to enable the transactional process.
FR3: The reward system shall enable rewards based on the user’s choice to view advertisements.

3.4 Community Management & Interactions
FR1: It shall give the opportunity to form groups with family and friends, sharing private interactions.
FR2: The system shall support forum creation based on the subject and notify participants when an event has occurred.
FR3: The system will offer messaging functionality where users will have direct private chats with their friends.

4. NON-FUNCTIONAL REQUIREMENTS
4.1 Reliability
NFR1: The system shall have 99.9% availability, excluding scheduled maintenance.
NFR2: The system shall ensure backup and recovery mechanisms in case of failure.

4.2 Usability
NFR3: The interface shall be intuitive and easy to use by people of any age.
NFR4: The system shall include search functionality enabling at least an 80% chance of easily locating a friend, content, or group.
NFR5: The system shall provide users with preference options to display content (newest, most popular) with instantaneous selections.

4.3 Performance
NFR6: The system shall be able to complete posting and content creation operations within 5 seconds under normal circumstances.
NFR7: The system shall ensure that at least 1000 users are supported simultaneously without any decline in performance.

4.4 Security
NFR8: The system shall ensure that user passwords and sensitive data are encrypted both in transit and at rest.
NFR9: The system shall have role-based access control that restricts actions according to user roles.
