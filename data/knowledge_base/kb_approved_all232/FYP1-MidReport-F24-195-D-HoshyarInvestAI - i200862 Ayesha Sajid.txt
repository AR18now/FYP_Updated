System Overview 
General Description 
HoshyarInvestAI is an AI -driven investment bot with a prospect to invest in Pakistan’s 
tech sector. It offers timely stock predictions, investment recommendations, and portfolio 
management tools to serve the needs of investors. It makes use of the data feeding in from 
multiple sources, such as financial reports and stock market data, and news articles with 
their respective sentiment analysis to provide investors with data -driven insights. The 
ultimate goal of the system is to assist individual and institutional investors in making 
effective choices regarding optimal investment strategies by using sophisticated machine 
learning models such as TFT and DRL. 
Features: The system enables access to real -time data for stocks, one can analyze mar - 
ket trends based on past trends, robust backtesting of various investment strategies, and 
personalized recommendations tailored to specific market conditions and user needs. 
Context: This is a system catering to fast -tech companies, which provides investment 
tools to optimize portfolios with insights beyond traditional investment metrics. Users 
may be on either the web application or mobile application, while administrators will be 
handling the backend data and models of the system.

3.3 Data Design 
 
In the HoshyarInvestAI project, the information domain has information related to stock 
data, financial reports, market trends, user profiles, and investment strategies. Here’s how 
the system transforms this domain into data structures and how the major entities are 
stored, processed, and organized as follows: 
1. Data Sources 
Stock Data: fetches real-time as well as past stock information, including share price, 
ticker symbols, and company-specific financial data. This information can be done either 
by fetching the APIs like PSX or through downloaded reports of the company’s financial 
statements. Market Trends: All economic trends, world events, and other macroeconomic 
indicators find their reflection in the security price. Financial Reports: PDFs, news arti - 
cles, and journal sources that carry critical company financials, quarterly reports, and 
investor sentiment. 
2. Data Structures and Entities Major Data Entities: 
Investor: It keeps personal data including name, e -mail, investment interest, and risk 
capacity. Structures Organized in structured data and stored in the database called User 
Data. Methods include setting investment strategies and viewing portfolios. 
Portfolio: 
It features live investments, historical performance, as well as information about invest - 
ment allocation. There are a variety of stocks in every portfolio and every portfolio has an 
investor behind it. It has been related that it is organized relational data in the User Data

Stock:

Data Collection:

Data Storage Items (Databases) 1-Stock Data:
