1.2 Scope
IlmPath is a platform where users can learn how to read the Holy Quran with tajweed.
Also, they can get more insights and knowledge about the Holy book. Plus, users can
interact with the ulama and Scholars to get knowledge on any specific topic. Now diving
deeper into the core features of our app. Starting from the first, users can learn how to
read the Holy Quran with tajweed step by step. The app also has a Quranic chatbot that
provides answers to queries based on the Quran. Moreover, users can recite any verse,
and it will identify its respective Surah and Ayah number. Our next module is a platform
where users can get connected to the ulama and scholars and get paid teachings from
them. The application also allows users to bookmark any verse and take notes. Users can
also track their learning performance to better analyze themselves. So these are all the
main functionalities of the project.
Now let’s discuss the boundaries and range of the proposed solution. IlmPath is mainly
focused on the learning of the Holy Quran and basic knowledge of it. Plus, it provides a
platform where users can get teachings from Ulama and Scholars. IlmPath does not cover
the whole teachings of Islam. It does not provide anything with respect to Hadith or any
other source of knowledge. It is mainly focused on learning the Quran and connecting
users with scholars for any specific topic.

In this module, the user will recite verses of the Holy Quran, and upon making a mistake,

The user will also be given an option to start learning the Quran from basics, in which the
user will be presented with Qaidah, and upon reading the Qaidah correctly, they will be
allowed to move forward to the Holy Quran.

System Overview
IlmPath aims to provide an experience that allows users to correct their pronunciation,
search queries and get answers in the light of the Quran, find Surahs and tackle any am-
biguity they have with the guidance of ullamas. This section provides an overview of
many designs that help understand this project and it’s functionalities. The section covers
illustrations of Architectural design, Activity Diagram, Class Diagram, Class-Level Se-
quence Diagram, State Transition Diagrams and an ERD to give representation of all the
functionalites and their workflow.
3.1 Architectural Design
Our project can best be represented in a Multi-Tiered Architecture that composes of three
layers. The first layer is the presentation layer that showcases the user interface i.e our
React Native application. The second layer contains all the business logic that will be
used to develop the application. This contains lessons and qirat testing, Quranic chatbot,
Surah finder, daily recitation and scholars platform, all part of the business logic. The
third and final layer encapsulates the data layer. This represents the models that process
data, database for storage and retrieval of data and payment gateways that ensure accurate
transactions.
