## 1. Introduction

Introduction 
Both LinkedIn, indeed and Glassdoor are recruitment sites that provide job post/positions 
and simple resume creation tools but do not have advanced and basic features, to enhance 
the users, feed -back loop. Most platforms to date are limited in functionality – resume 
builders are mainly concerned with design and minimal content, while the job boards 
make their search based on keywords. That is why candidates are not always offered a 
job they desire because their resumes may not be optimized or the matching has been 
done improperly. 
Numerous products, such as Jobscan and Rezi, make users more ATS-friendly but do not 
connect job suggestions or career planning, leaving users trapped in a web of non -
intersecting tools for a highly interconnected job world. This is most un -strategic in 
terminals as it makes it hard for applicants in their job search to leverage on their 
applications the best way possible as they seek the next career step. 
To fill these gaps, GenResume covers resume enhancement, job search, skills assessment, 
and career advice all in one. With features of artificial intelligence, GenResume seeks to 
be an all-inclusive support service towards job searching. 
 
 
1.1 Problem Statement 
 
CV creation for the ATS specialists is a major hurdle for the job seekers, the process of 
job search for the right fit is equally a problem and the need-to-know what skills one lacks 
for the desired job is another challenge. Currently available tools cover only one or two 
steps in the job application process for example formatting of resume or, job search but 
lacks an over- arching solution. This disconnection results in job seekers being unable to 
enhance application documents, match themselves with demand and manage career 
transitions. 
This way, GenResume provides the ability to create unique, ATS -friendly resumes for 
the intended job title, recommend jobs from the external databases for the user, and 
highlight possible gaps in the user’s skillset, within the same service.

1.2 Scope 
 
The areas of GenResume include several aspects of the job application process, and 
its primary goal is to help job seekers find a job with tools that are not available on 
other platforms. The platform includes: 
User Profile Creation and Customization : Users can input all their experience, 
education, skills, credentials, and projects in an effort to put together a 
comprehensive profile. This information builds the foundation for creating resumes 
and getting job recommendations. 
ATS-Compliant Resume Generation: It is possible to create an individual resume 
and, on its basis, develop an ATS -friendly version. Once the candidate picks a 
particular job title from suggested job ad posting, the system then produces a resume 
with the right keywords and structures to beat the ATS scans. The specialized 
platform offers guidance on how well the resume fits the position – including 
changes that may be made. 
Job Search and Personalized Recommendations : The GenResume also links 
candidates to external job boards such as Indeed and Glassdoor; the results are 
filtered according to the user’s profile. These recommendations are not plain 
keyword matches; instead, it aligns the user’s profile to the job by using AI as a tool 
for comparison. 
Skill Gap Analysis and Learning Resources: The above listed are unique features 
and by analyzing job descriptions of various positions, the skills and qualifications 
not matched with the user profile is brought out. It then offers concrete suggestions 
to use these services such as Coursera and edX to bridge these gaps for users. 
Career Path Guidance and Job Market Trends : GenResume is an application 
that will assist users in mapping out their career progressions as it possesses various 
real-time job market information. This feature makes user aware of the 
expectations, emerging positions, and requirements that are valued in a particular 
field.

1.3 Modules 
 
1.3.1 User Profile Creation & Data Management 
 
The first unit focuses on the prerequisites of constructing and curating user profiles 
and the data associated with it. This module is designed specially to collect user data 
and structure it for the sake of other operation. 
Features: 
1. Manual Profile Creation: To provide information about the professional 
background, users can enter their educational and workplace related data, 
skills, certifications, and projects. 
2. Data Management System: Well-organized and easily accessible system of 
maintaining the data about users with the possibility of their profile updates. 
3. GitHub Integration: It also supports users to link the GitHub account to 
import the entire data automatically while creating the profile. 
4. Profile Editing: Users can update their profiles with new skills, 
certifications, or experience as they progress in their careers. 
 
1.3.2 Basic Resume Generation & Job Aggregation 
 
• This module builds upon the user profile to enable resume generation and job 
aggregation functionalities. 
 Features: 
 
1. Basic Resume Generation: Users can generate resumes using predefined 
templates. These resumes will pull data from the user’s profile to create tai - 
lored documents. 
2. Multiple ATS-Compliant Templates: Offering a variety of templates that 
comply with ATS guidelines to improve the chances of resumes passing 
through automated systems. 
3. Job Aggregation: Integration with job boards (e.g., LinkedIn, Indeed, Glass- 
door) to aggregate job listings relevant to the user’s profile. 
4. Job Search Functionality: Basic search features that allow users to search 
for jobs by role, location, or company.

1.3.3 Advanced Role-Based Insights & Skill Matching 
 
• This module introduces advanced role -based insights and skill matching 
features, enhancing the user’s ability to target specific roles and identify areas 
for improvement. 
 Features: 
 
1. Role-Based Career Insights: Providing users with detailed analysis on the 
skills, experience, and qualifications needed for specific roles, such as AI/ML 
Engineer, Data Scientist, etc. 
2. Skill Matching: Comparing user profiles with job descriptions to identify 
matching skills and highlight areas where the user may need further develop- 
ment. 
3. Skill Insights: Providing more descriptive knowledge about a given skill, its 
applicability, how it is used in the market and the occupations related to it. 
4. Career Path Analysis: Proposing the actual and possible training and 
development programs according to the user’s existing competencies and 
target occupation. 
1.3.4 Basic Resume Generation & Job Aggregation 
 
• The last one is dedicated to the supplement to the platform and utilizes the integration 
of AI and NLP to improve core functions of a platform. 
 Features: 
 
1. AI-Powered Resume Generation: Applying natural language processing 
technology in order to read a job description and create an original resume 
that corresponds to all the conditions. 
2. NLP-Based Job Description Analysis: For instance, using NLP to 
summarize and analyze job descriptions so that the user benefits by finding 
out what the job really implies. 
3. Advanced Skill Gap Analysis: An application of artificial intelligence for 
the purpose of estimating the time and work experience necessary for training 
according to the profile established by the user and his/her goals. 
4. AI-Driven Job Recommendations: Matching users with job offers 
according to the information provided by the users and their submitted 
applications.
