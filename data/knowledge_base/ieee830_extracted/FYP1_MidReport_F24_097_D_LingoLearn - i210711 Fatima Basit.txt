1.1 Problem Statement
Language is still a considerable barrier that hinders communication in education, business
and daily life[11], especially for students who apply for scholarships in foreign countries
where language proficiency is a requirement. Even with an abundance of language learn-
ing apps out there, many are boring and unengaging, making it hard for people to stay
motivated when they’re trying to learn a new language. These apps also lack real-time
scenario practice or on-the-fly pronunciation feedback, which is so essential for learning
a language. Live sessions with native speakers can be expensive or difficult to schedule
due to timezone issues, reducing one of the greatest advantages — flexibility. Our sys-
tem helps overcome these shortcomings by creating a sensory and cognitively immersive
language learning experience with AR, on top of AI. This provides practice for real-life
situations, instant pronunciation feedback, and communication with a virtual language
partner in the learner’s own time. We want to evolve from the shortcomings of tradi-
tional apps and provide an engaging, affordable, practical alternative that leads users back
on their journey toward achieving language fluency without class or financial time con-
straints. [22]
1.2 Scope
This project involves the creation of a mobile language learning app that will leverage
Augmented Reality (AR) and Artificial Intelligence (AI). Key features of the app include
a Mobile Augmented Reality (MAR), which takes users into 3D real-world situations and
immerses them in cultural language contexts for learning. A user can use his phone and
explore an AR environment which improves learning vastly, making the experience more
intuitive and unforgettable. An AI-powered language accompaniment is a personalized
tutor offering real-time guidance and natural practice conversations. The app comes with
enhanced speech recognition that gives feedback on your pronunciation, so you can work
on speaking practice in the best possible way. It also includes real-world scenario-based
final assessments to evaluate whether the learner can put their language skills to use like
an example of ordering food or traveling through a foreign market. Track your progress
and performance with analytics so that you stay driven throughout the learning process,
instilling a sense of streaks along with personalized feedback. Students business profes-
sionals, working employees, and even tourists- all can achieve their requirements with
this platform. It starts with users in Pakistan, the UK, and Germany — but there are plans

1.4 User Classes and Characteristics
User class Description
Students Students are individuals who are registered within some organization, par-
ticularly institutions of learning, and are interested in language acquisition.
The application might be used in enhancing one’s studies or even learning
personally. It is anticipated that students will always turn to the applica-
tion on a regular basis for lessons, pronunciation practice, and AR-based
scenario exercises. Numerous learners are likely to utilize the application
for examinations or for reasons such as scholarship requirements, and they
expect clear progress tracking. They would use AR rich modules and AI
teaching avatar to enhance their experience.
Tourists Tourists are occasional learners who want to grasp basic conversational
skills in a foreign language for travel purposes. They will typically use
this mobile application to learn phrases from the AI tutor and will be more
inclined to use the app to prepare for specific situations like ordering food,
asking for directions, or navigating foreign markets. For tourists, the fo-
cus of interactions through the app will be the AR environments rather than
learning languages in other cultures.
Staff Staff members are people who will seek to embark on language acquisi-
tion as one way of improving for better job performance, promotions or
enhancement of workplace integration. These users may be using the app in
the office where they try to incorporate learning while going about their day
to day tasks.
Language
Enthusiasts
These users are highly motivated who intend to acquire proficiency in vari-
ous languages and also further their linguistic skills in one language. Such
users will make use of all the functionalities which are given to them in-
cluding the advanced AR module and in depth lessons. Enthusiasts can be
expected to change languages quite often, and due to that, they will want to
be able to switch languages seamlessly. This class of users will also use AI
companion for personalized learning experiences and they expect the system
to be reliable and offer challenging tasks and tests, as they are likely to be
committed persistent users.
